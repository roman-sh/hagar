Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

1. Overview

Teivah Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") provides automated messaging services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services, including our integration with the WhatsApp Cloud API.

2. Data Processing

Our application integrates with the WhatsApp Cloud API to provide automated messaging capabilities. We process the following types of data:

  • Message Content: Text and media sent via our service.
  • Metadata: Phone numbers, timestamps, and message delivery status.

3. Data Usage

We use your data solely for the purpose of operating and maintaining our messaging services. Specifically:

  • To deliver automated responses and notifications.
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and ensure service stability.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data or message content to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Message logs are automatically purged periodically.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Our service relies on the Meta/WhatsApp Cloud API infrastructure. Data processed through WhatsApp is subject to Meta's privacy practices as the platform provider. We do not share your data with any other third parties unless required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about the data we hold about you or request its deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us.
